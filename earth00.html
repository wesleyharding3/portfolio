earth00

News intelligence — website, iOS app & daily briefings · personal project · 2025– · earth00.com ↗

earth00 reads thousands of news stories in 48 languages and figures out which ones are about the same event. The hard part isn't collecting the news — it's realizing that an article in Mandarin and one in Arabic are the same story, when they don't share a single word.

It's running right now — the real site, sandboxed. Spin the globe.

Пекин  ·  北京  ·  بكين — the same city, written three ways, recognized as one: beijing. That's the whole trick, at scale.

The result is one picture of the world's news: a of where coverage is happening, a that groups hundreds of articles into events, and an that carries it all in a pocket.

And people watch: a daily briefing is generated and auto-published across platforms — about 50,000 combined impressions a week, without a hand on the wheel.

Under the hood
Stack
Node.js · PostgreSQL + pgvector · Claude Haiku · DeepL · local transformers · Three.js
Read
Script-aware extraction across Latin, Cyrillic, Arabic, and CJK; a frequency prefilter drops ~45% of language-model calls before they happen.
Normalize
Non-English keywords map to one canonical English form via a three-layer cascade — local list, then Wikidata, then the model. About $1.80 a day at steady state.
Group
A local CPU model embeds every article at $0 per run; nearest-neighbor search clusters coverage into story threads.
Scale
~266K articles per cycle · 72 tables · 24 scheduled workers · ships as a native iOS app
