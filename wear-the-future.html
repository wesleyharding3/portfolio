storefront

Shopify storefront · 2026 · wear-thefuture.com ↗

Wear the Future is a Los Angeles fashion house. Its showroom runs on the inventory system I built; this is its public face — a Shopify storefront that turns the showroom's physical collections into a shoppable catalog, brand by brand.

The introduces the house; the presents each brand's pieces — the same garments the showroom tracks, sold to the public.

The site is browsable in the window: its nav bar is clickable, page to page, exactly as it is live.

Together with the inventory system, the two sides close a loop: the rack in the showroom and the catalog on the web describe the same physical collection, and neither drifts from the other.
