Shopify storefront — design & build · 2026 · bealice.com ↗

BEALICE is a high-fashion handbag label based in Minnesota. I designed the site — the typography, the pacing, the whole feel — and built it on Shopify, so the label runs its own store without a developer on call.

The leads with the bags and stays out of their way; the keep the grid quiet so the product photography carries the room. Where the inventory work below is about correctness, a storefront is about feel — type, imagery, and rhythm doing the selling.

The store is browsable in the window: its own menu and navigation are clickable, page to page, exactly as they are live.

Shopify handles checkout, payments, and fulfillment; the design layer is custom. The label updates products, prices, and campaigns itself.
